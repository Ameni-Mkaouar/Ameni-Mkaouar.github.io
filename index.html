Bio

Dr. Mkaouar is a Postdoctoral Research Associate at GESTAR II, University of Maryland, Baltimore County (UMBC), and NASA Goddard Space Flight Center. Her research focuses on advanced remote sensing techniques and the development of models to analyze forest ecosystems and their structure, with a special interest in forest biomass estimation, carbon modeling, and remote sensing data fusion.

Research Interests

  • LiDAR and photogrammetry for environmental monitoring
  • Forest structure and biomass estimation
  • Data fusion and machine learning applications
  • Climate change modeling and carbon flux
